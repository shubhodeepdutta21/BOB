# GREY# 🤖 BOB: The Smart DIY Platform

![BOB Platform Cover](public/your-cover-image.png) **Turn your spare parts into brilliant projects.** BOB is an innovative platform designed to empower students and makers with limited resources. Tell us what hardware you have lying around, and we will tell you exactly what you can build. Stop watching tutorials for projects you don't have the parts for.

## ✨ Key Features

* **📦 Smart Inventory Management:** Easily log your microcontrollers, sensors, and power supplies. Your digital workshop follows you everywhere.
* **🎯 Match Readiness Engine:** Our algorithm scans your inventory against a database of 200+ DIY projects, showing you exactly what you can build right now with a "Match Readiness" percentage.
* **🧠 AI Auto-Invent (Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash):** Have a weird combination of parts? Our AI Agent will instantly invent a brand new, safe, and working DIY project using *only* the exact components you currently own.
* **☁️ Cloud Sync & Auth:** Secure login via Google or Email/Password. Your inventory is safely synced to the cloud so you never lose your progress.
* **🎨 Premium 3D UI/UX:** Built with a stunning, responsive dark-mode interface featuring interactive dot-grids and embedded 3D Spline models.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS
* **Backend & Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth)
* **Artificial Intelligence:** Google GenAI SDK (`gemini-2.5-flash`)
* **UI/Icons:** Lucide React, Spline 3D
